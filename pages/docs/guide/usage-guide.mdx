# Usage Guide

This guide provides an overview of how to use the Expense Tracker app.

<Steps>
  ### Step 1: Sign Up or Log In

To start using the app, create a new account or log in with your existing credentials. Use your email or social login.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Monthly Budget

Navigate to **Settings > Budget** and enter your expected income and budget for the month. This will help you track your financial goals effectively.

### Step 3: Add Your First Expense

Go to the **Expenses** section, click on "Add Expense", and fill in the details such as amount, category, and date to log your spending.

### Step 4: Monitor Your Spending

Use the **Dashboard** to see an overview of your expenses, remaining budget, and trends over time. This helps you keep track of your finances.

### Step 5: Generate Reports

Visit the **Reports** section to create monthly or custom reports. You can export data as CSV or PDF for detailed analysis.

</Steps>

## Tips for Effective Usage

- **Set Realistic Budgets:** Start with achievable goals to avoid overspending.
- **Regularly Log Expenses:** Enter expenses as they occur to maintain accurate records.
- **Review Reports Monthly:** Analyze reports to understand spending patterns and adjust your budget accordingly.
